What is WSC?
Work|Studying|Chilling is an online studying environment built around students, and created as a safe space for peers to be able to exchange ideas, receive help, and tutor others on various subjects, ranging from AP courses to SAT prep.

How does it work?

We use chatrooms to allow members to easily communicate with one another in dedicated channels. In addition, voice channels are also available, making collaborating or chatting with others easier than ever!

What else can you do?
Not only are we a friendly place to study in, but we're also a really fun place to just hang out in! From music bots and memes to hobby channels, we are truly made for all three -- work, studying, and chilling!
